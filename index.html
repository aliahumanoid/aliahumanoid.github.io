About Alia

Alia is a 175 cm female humanoid robot, designed to integrate all components — joints, tendons, motors — within the realistic volumetry of a human body.

3D printed in PA12 with a TPU shell, Alia uses motorized joints with QDD architecture and UHMWPE tendon transmission for natural, sophisticated movement.

Our Approach

Biomechanical Integration

Every component is designed to live in harmony with the human form, not just functionality but expression of identity through movement.

Advanced Materials

PA12 for internal structure, TPU for external shell, UHMWPE for tendon transmission systems.

Human-Like Motion

QDD architecture and distributed control for natural, sophisticated movement patterns.
