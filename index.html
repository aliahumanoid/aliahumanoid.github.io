About Alia

Phase 0 – Research & Exploration. Alia is an engineering research project exploring the mechanical challenges of human-like motion in humanoid robotics.

We're investigating how joints, actuation systems, and materials can work together to achieve natural, sophisticated movement patterns. This is not a commercial product—it's a technical exploration documented transparently as it evolves.

Follow our progress via Public Updates and technical logs.

Our Approach

Biomechanical Integration

Every component is designed to live in harmony with the human form, not just functionality but expression of identity through movement.

Advanced Materials

PA12 for internal structure, TPU for external shell, UHMWPE for tendon transmission systems.

Human-Like Motion

QDD architecture and distributed control for natural, sophisticated movement patterns.

Licensing Roadmap
